Our Vision

At Blitz, we have a simple vision: become the leading payment solutions platform in Bitcoin with the underlying goal to bring Bitcoin payments to the masses. To do this, we are focusing on two things: usability and abstraction.

Usabilty

To us, usability means anyone from being an expert in Bitcoin to your grandmother can easily use the app. We are striving to achieve this usability through minimalistic design, extensive user testing, and abstraction of technical features.

Abstraction

A user should not need to understand how a product works to use it. Knowing this, we have removed the complexities of the different technologies we use to simplify the experience for the user.

Purpose

To empower users to send and receive payments across the globe with ease.

People

Create a simple, KYC free, and self-custodial application to allow people to transact freely and securly.

Planet

Remove payment barriors, and economic ineqalities between regions

Transparency

Utilize the open-source philosophy in all buinsess processes such as idea generation, code, and future plans.
