Our Bitcoin Thesis

At Blitz, we view Bitcoin as a currency. In order to transform Bitcoin into that currency, two main things need to happen: Bitcoin needs to become easier to use, Bitcoin needs to be accepted in stores. Since Blitz is a consumer facing product, meaing we focus on the customer side of the equasion, our focus is to make Bitcoin extremly simple to use.
